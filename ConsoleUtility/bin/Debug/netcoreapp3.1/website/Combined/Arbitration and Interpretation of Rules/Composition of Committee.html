11.2 Composition of Committee

11.2.1 (ActionAir | Handgun only)
The composition of the Arbitration Committee will be subject to the following rules:
11.2.1.1 (ActionAir | Handgun only)
The IPSC President, or his delegate, or a certified Match Official appointed by the Match Director, (in that order) will serve as Chairman of the committee with no vote.
11.2.1.2 (ActionAir | Handgun only)
Three arbitrators will be appointed by the IPSC President, or his delegate, or by the Match Director, (in that order), with one vote each.
11.2.1.3 (ActionAir | Handgun only)
When possible arbitrators should be competitors in the match and should be certified Match Officials.
11.2.1.4 (ActionAir | Handgun only)
Under no circumstances must the Chairman or any member of an Arbitration Committee be a party to the original decision or subsequent appeals, which led to the arbitration.
11.2.2 Level I and II Matches
The Match Director can appoint an Arbitration Committee of three experienced persons who are not parties to the appeal and who do not have a direct conflict of interest in the outcome of the appeal. The arbitrators should be certified Match Officials if possible. All committee members will vote. The senior Match Official, or the senior person if there are no Match Officials, will be the chairman.
